Food Safety Tips

1. Wash your hands thoroughly before handling food.

2. Keep raw meats separate from other foods to avoid cross-contamination.

3. Cook food to the recommended internal temperatures.

4. Refrigerate perishable foods promptly.

5. Use different cutting boards for raw and cooked foods.
